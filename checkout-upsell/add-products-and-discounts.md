# Add Products & Set Discounts

In **Step 2** of the offer editor, you select which products to recommend and set an optional discount for each.

## Adding products

1. Click **Add product**
2. Search and select products from the Shopify Product Picker
3. Choose specific variants or keep all variants selected
4. Click **Add** to confirm

You can add multiple products to a single offer. Each product is displayed as a separate card in the upsell block.

## Setting a discount

Each product can have its own discount, independent of other products in the same offer.

| Discount Type | Description | Example |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| **Percentage** | Reduce price by a percentage | 10% off |
| **Fixed amount** | Reduce price by a fixed value | $5 off |
| **No discount** | Show at full price | — |

Discounts are applied automatically via Shopify's Automatic Discount system when the buyer clicks **Add**.

## Reordering products

Drag the handle (six dots) on the left of a product card to change the display order. The first product in the list appears first in the upsell block at checkout.

## Editing or removing a product

- Click the **edit icon** on a product card to change variants or discount
- Click the **trash icon** to remove the product from the offer

{% hint style="info" %}
Discounts are created and synced to Shopify automatically when you save an active offer. They are removed when the offer is deactivated, deleted, or expired.
{% endhint %}
