# Manage Priority

When multiple offers have matching conditions at checkout, priority determines which offer is displayed first.

## How Priority Works

- Each offer has a **priority number** (1 = highest priority)
- When a buyer reaches checkout, all active offers are evaluated
- Offers whose conditions match are displayed in priority order
- All matching offers are shown — priority controls the order, not visibility

## Setting Priority

1. Go to **Offers** in the left menu
2. Click **Manage priority**
3. In the modal, select **Checkout upsell** from the dropdown
4. Drag and drop offers to reorder them
5. Click **Save**

The priority numbers update automatically based on the new order.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Tip:** Put your highest-converting or most time-sensitive offers at the top (priority 1). Buyers see these first, so they have the best chance of being noticed.
{% endhint %}
