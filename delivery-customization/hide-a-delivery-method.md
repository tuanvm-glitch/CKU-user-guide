# Hide a Delivery Method

Use this rule to remove specific shipping options from checkout when conditions are met.

## When to Use

- Hide express shipping for digital products or lightweight items
- Hide local pickup for buyers outside your delivery zone
- Show only one specific method for certain product types

## Setup

1. Go to **Checkout Customization** → **Delivery** tab
2. Click **Create rule** → select **Hide delivery method**
3. Set a **Rule name** and **Status** (Active / Inactive)
4. Configure **Trigger Conditions** (optional)
5. In the **Rule Action** section, choose what to hide:
   - **Hide all** — Remove all delivery methods
   - **Hide specific methods** — Select methods by name
   - **Hide by type** — Hide Shipping, Pickup, or Local delivery as a group
   - **Show only specific methods** — Hide everything except selected methods
6. Click **Save**
