# Deal Badge

{% hint style="success" %}
Deal Badge is used for Buy X Get Y campaign only.
{% endhint %}

### What is Deal Badge?

The Deal Badge is a visual icon or label that appears on product images, highlighting special offers like discounts, free gifts or other exclusive incentives. It's strategically placed to immediately catch customers' attention and clearly communicate any active promotions tied to specific products.

### Why to use Deal Badge?

<details>

<summary>Draw Immediate Attention to Offers</summary>

An effective Deal Badge catches the customer's eyes right away to special offers and promotions.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Drives sales and Boosts AOV</summary>

By reducing the need for customers to search for deals, a Deal Badge helps streamline the shopping exprience and boost conversions. It also serves a effective triggers for these spur-of-the-moment purchases, which can contribute to higher AOV.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Improve Customer Experience</summary>

A Deal Badge will make it easier for customers to spot promotions. It offers a quick way to identify the best deals without requiring customers to hunt for promotions in separate section, thereby enhancing their shopping experience.

</details>

### How to set up Deal Badge to your store?

#### Step 1: Access the Deal Badge Settings

Navigate **Widget Display** > **Deal Badge** in the dashboard. Choose **Setup** to start customize it.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FEWbwdjyZLEaA3X0kaEWb%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=1d412d22-af31-4f51-b89a-062735d69a3c" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Step 2: Choose a Widget Theme

In the **Widget Theme** section, you can select a theme that aligns with your store’s branding.

The app offers both **Regular Themes** and **Seasonal Themes** to keep your promotions fresh and relevant.

* **Regular Themes** include options like _Winter, Forest, Ocean, Twilight, Midnight, Dusk, Summer,_ and _Spring_.
* **Seasonal Themes** allow you to adjust badges for seasonal campaigns, which can be particularly effective during holiday seasons.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FTMFR90wFYEZhrxhyZVwk%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=31661e82-2d8f-4c2b-a400-96b30066ff60" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click on your preferred theme, and the preview will update to show how it looks in real-time.

#### Step 3: Set up Widget style

Decide how the Widget will be displayed on Homepage & Collection page.

Under **Badge Position**, choose where you'd like the badge to appear on your product images:

* Options include _Top Left, Top Right, Bottom Left,_ and _Bottom Right._

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FDYgLOTU3XG9kbQfE3FXB%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=1a2ca998-0870-4002-9edc-9ee222d711cb" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Preview the placement in the panel on the right to ensure it fits well with your store layout.

#### Customize the Background

Choose whether you want a **Gradient Background** or a solid color.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FVgwqULqkdfALguid4pPA%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=1aec19ba-5a69-40cb-8e92-14a65afc9480" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Adjust the Text Color**

In the **Text** section, select a **Text Color** that matches your store's theme and stands out against the background.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2F0GmFtw1MBwL3MxzHEkGr%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d75c1208-3b63-4187-9748-170eefa28058" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Choose an Icon

In the **Icon** section, you can choose from various icons to display within the badge.

Then, customize its color to make sure it match the overall theme.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FlpLR3LdfHo91izw7xKAT%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=5c17e6f0-2c42-4838-8c5a-d7b09fc1cdb2" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Final step:

Preview the Deal Badge by seeing how it will appear to customers on the right sight of the settings.

After verifying that everything works as expected, click **Save**.

Implementing these eye-catching deal badges is a quick win for enhancing your store's visual appeal and encouraging more sales. Start using the Deal Badge feature today to make your promotions stand out!
