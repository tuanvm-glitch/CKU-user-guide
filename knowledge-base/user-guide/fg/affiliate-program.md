# Affiliate program

This guide will help you register, join the **AOV.ai Free Gift** affiliate campaign, and start sharing your affiliate link to earn commissions.

### Step 1: Register an affiliate account

1. Go to the Avada Affiliate Portal:\
   [**https://partners.avada.io/affiliate/login**](https://partners.avada.io/affiliate/login)
2. Click **Register**.
3. Fill in the required information:
   * **Email** (used for login & verification)
   * **PayPal Email** (optional, for receiving payouts)
   * **Password** & **Confirm Password**
4. Click **Create Account** and verify your email.

Once completed, you’ll be logged into the affiliate dashboard.

***

### Step 2: Join the AOV Free Gift Affiliate Campaign

1. From the dashboard, click **Browse New Campaigns**.
2. Find **AOV Free Gift** in the campaign list.
3. Click **Join campaign**.

🎉 After joining, the campaign status will change to **Joined**.

**Commission details (example):**

* **30% lifetime commission**
* Applies to plan upgrades (Basic, Advanced, Plus, Shopify plans)

***

### Step 3: Copy your affiliate link

1. Go to **My Campaigns**.
2. Find **AOV Free Gift** under **My Active Campaigns**.
3. Click **Copy Link**.

🔗 This is your unique affiliate link.

### Step 4: Start referring merchants

You can now share your affiliate link via:

* Blog posts
* YouTube videos
* TikTok / Facebook / X
* Shopify community, Discord, Slack
* Direct referrals to merchants

You can track:

* Clicks
* Installs
* Conversion rate
* Earnings directly in the **My Campaigns** dashboard.

***

### You’re All Set 🚀

That’s it!\
You’ve successfully joined the **AOV.ai Free Gift Affiliate Program** and can start earning commissions by referring Shopify merchants.
