# Volume Discount

### Volume Discount feature migration

The Volume Discount feature previously available in the AOV Free Gift app has now been fully migrated to our **AOV Bundle Upsell** app. This change is part of our efforts to consolidate functionalities and provide a more seamless and powerful upsell experience.

🔄 **What this means for you**\
While you will still be able to see previously created Volume Discount campaigns inside the AOV Free Gift app, **you will no longer be able to edit campaign settings or customize the widget layout**.

👉 To continue using the Volume Discount feature and ensure full feature access and the best user experience, **please install the AOV Bundle Upsell** app here: [https://apps.shopify.com/aov-bundle-upsell](https://apps.shopify.com/aov-bundle-upsell)

The AOV Bundle app includes an enhanced version of the Volume Discount campaign, along with other powerful bundling and upsell features designed to maximize your AOV.

***

### What is Volume Discount campaign?

Volume Discount allows you to set up discounts that lower the price of the items bought when your customers increase quantities they purchase. It encourages larger purchases and gives your customers a reason to add more items to their cart while maximizing your revenue.

### How to set up Volume Discount campaign?

#### Step 1: Select the campaign

In the dashboard of your app, choose **Create campaign** > **Volume Discount** from the list of campaign types to start create one.

#### **Step 2: Set up Campaign name**

In **General** section, give your campaign a name. This name is only used for internal viewing and won't be visible to customers. For example, you might call it "Volume Discount #1"

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FxStqEZGO6zQQ0H00YTvF%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d00ab496-e203-48dd-90e6-6b1df7ea7dcb" alt=""><figcaption><p>Volume Discount general setting</p></figcaption></figure>

#### **Step 3: Set up Campaign Trigger**

**Trigger Condition**

Select whether you want to apply the discount to:

* **Specific product:** Choose this if the discount applies only to one or more specific products.
* **Specific collection**: Choose this if the discount applies to a certain collection in your store.

Click **Browse** and select the applicable product(s) or collection(s).

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FJ7suQteZs2nWl4bBR2To%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a822640f-fc9e-4111-8792-c85f27567bc0" alt=""><figcaption><p>Volume Discount Campaign trigger setting</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Step 4: Set up Campaign offer

In the Campaign offer section, you can set your discount tiers with specific product quantity and according discounts.

**Discount type**

Choose the type of discount applied for all tiers. It can be Percentage or Fixed amount off.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2F8CFLyDPiNPPLu68ix8sC%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=341a383e-513f-4941-873d-d23144235baa" alt=""><figcaption><p>Volume Discount type setting</p></figcaption></figure>

For each tier, decide the product quantity and discount value accordingly.

You can add more tier as needed by clicking **+Add tier**

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2F8qKfLz3hzQRRBUoN1mdY%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=40e9dcf7-7aa0-4187-8225-fe84bbd974e6" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To highlight a default tier (e.g., Tier 2 in the screenshot), click on Actions next to the tier and select the **Default Option**. This will display the most popular discount tier to customers with a highlight.

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FXTJO0rDNrQPmlRKk4QkT%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=60b0b232-6223-4906-a3f9-0d9cdeacc42d" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Discount combinations**

You can totally let your customers combine this discount with other different types to let them have the best deal based on their cart. Other discounts you can choose to combine are:

* Product discounts
* Shipping discounts
* Order discounts

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2FCopz4riLl9A8l34JBDAx%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e79e0f12-01d5-44e6-aee5-693006d37f60" alt=""><figcaption><p>Volume Discount combination setting</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Step 5: Set up Volume Widget

In the **Volume Widget** section, you can customize the display text that your customers will see on your store. Make sure to Enable Widget embed in your theme first.

Set the **Title** (e.g., "Get More, Save More") to create a compelling call-to-action.

Write a short **Description** that explains the benefit of the discount (e.g., "Unlock greater savings as you purchase more items").

<figure><img src="https://1289686769-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMo81Ak7Ig0Z6bLRmWpSA%2Fuploads%2F4cfjmb6cuiTXbWXzZFq1%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=f8cf4946-8b13-4e8d-9009-175107b34d30" alt=""><figcaption><p>Volume Discount widget setting</p></figcaption></figure>

Preview how the widget will appear to customers on the right side of the screen.

#### Step 6 (Optional): Set up Schedule

If you want your promotion to run for a specific period of time, you can totally set the start and end date/ time. Notice that timezone is based on (GNT - 5:00) America/ New York.

**Start date**

Add the start date and time when your promotion will begin.

**End date (Optional)**

Add the end date and time when your promotion will stop.

#### Step 7 (Optional): Advanced settings

#### Specific link

Refer to this article to set up a specific link so that you can control who can access to your gift promotional campaign(s).

#### Final step

After configuring all settings, review your campaign settings to ensure everything is correct.

Once satisfied, click **Save** at the bottom of the page to launch your Volume Discount campaign.

And that's it! You've successfully set up the Volume Discount campaign and create a captivating experience for your customers. Happy selling!
