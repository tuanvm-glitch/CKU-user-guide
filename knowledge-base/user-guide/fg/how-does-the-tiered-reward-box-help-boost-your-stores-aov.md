# How Does the Tiered Reward Box Help Boost Your Store's AOV?

#### What is the Tiered Reward Box?

The Tiered Reward Box is a widget that appears as a floating element on your store, providing a visually appealing way to highlight ongoing promotions. Specifically, it focuses on tiered gift campaigns—promotions where customers unlock rewards based on their spending levels. The more they spend, the more they receive, creating a compelling incentive for higher cart values.

#### How Does It Work?

1. **Tiered Campaign Display**: highlights your store's tiered gift campaigns, showing customers exactly how much they need to spend to unlock different levels of rewards.\
   For example: "Spend $50 to get a free keychain," "Spend $100 to receive a gift card," and "Spend $200 to unlock a premium gift."
2. **Real-Time Progress Tracking**: As customers add items to their cart, the Gift Box updates in real-time, showing how close they are to reaching the next tier. This feature creates a sense of urgency and excitement, motivating customers to add just one more item to their cart to qualify for the next reward.
3. **Seamless Integration**: The tiered reward box integrates smoothly with your existing store design, ensuring it complements your brand without overwhelming the user experience. It’s customizable to match your store’s look and feel, making it a natural extension of your site.

#### Why Use the Tiered Reward Box?

* **Boosts Sales**: By encouraging customers to spend more to reach the next reward tier, the Tiered Reward Box naturally increases your average order value.
* **Enhances Customer Engagement**: The interactive nature of the widget keeps customers engaged throughout their shopping journey, making their experience more enjoyable and rewarding.
* **Increases Conversion Rates**: With clear, compelling offers presented right at the point of decision, the Tiered Reward Box helps turn browsers into buyers.
* **Supports Marketing Campaigns**: Whether it’s a seasonal promotion or a limited-time offer, the Tiered Reward Box is a powerful way to highlight and support your marketing efforts.

#### Conclusion

The Tiered Reward Box is more than just a widget—it’s a game-changer for online stores looking to maximize sales and customer engagement. By showcasing tiered gift campaigns and encouraging customers to spend more to get more, the Tiered Reward Box helps you create a win-win situation: customers receive exciting rewards, and you enjoy higher sales and increased brand loyalty.
