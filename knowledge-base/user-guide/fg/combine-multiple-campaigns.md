# Combine multiple campaigns

As a merchant, you might wonder whether running just one promotional campaign is enough to engage your customers and boost sales. The answer?

Multiple campaigns often create more impact. By combining different types of promotions, you can reach a broader audience, increase customer engagement, and drive higher sales.

### 1. Why Multiple Campaigns Work Better

Studies and industry insights suggest that merchants who run more than one type of promotional campaign often see better overall results. This is because different customers are drawn to different types of incentives. While one customer may be motivated by a discount, another might respond better to a free gift or volume-based offer.

Using a mix of promotional strategies helps to capture the attention of a wider audience and increases the chances of converting them into loyal buyers.

Moreover, combining campaigns can also encourage customers to add more items to their cart, ultimately increasing the average order value. When you provide multiple opportunities for customers to benefit, they’re more likely to take advantage of your offers.
