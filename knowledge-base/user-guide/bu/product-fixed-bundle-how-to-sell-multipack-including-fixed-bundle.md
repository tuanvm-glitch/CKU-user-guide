# How to sell multipack including fixed bundle?

This guide will take you through the steps of creating multipack options, which can boost sales by motivating customers to buy in larger quantities, often at a discounted price.

### What is a Multipack Bundle?&#x20;

Multipack selling allows you to combine multiple units of a fixed product bundle into a single package, sold as one. Customers who purchase the multipack get a better deal than if they bought the items individually.&#x20;

### Sample

Assume there is a bundle (1 pack) consisting of 2 pairs of red socks and 3 pairs of blue socks priced at $30. The table below shows the calculation if you want to sell 5 packs with a 10% discount and 10 packs with a 20% discount.

| Quantity | Red socks                    | Blue socks                  | Discount | Price                        |
| -------- | ---------------------------- | --------------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------- |
| 1 pack   | 2 pairs                      | 3 pairs                     | 0        | $30                          |
| 5 packs  | <p>10 pairs<br>(= 5*2)</p>   | <p>15 pairs<br>(= 5*3)</p>  | 10%      | <p>$135<br>(= 5*30*90%)</p>  |
| 10 packs | <p>20 pairs <br>(= 10*2)</p> | <p>30 pairs<br>(= 10*3)</p> | 20%      | <p>$240<br>(= 10*30*80%)</p> |

#### 1. Create and Activate Your Product Fixed Bundle&#x20;

* Once you have defined the contents of a bundle unit, you [create a **Product Fixed Bundle**](how-to-set-up) so that each unit within the bundle is treated as a standalone product.
* To meet the requirements for setting up multipack selling, please ensure that your recently saved product is active.

#### 3. Set-up for Multipack selling

Step 1: Enable 'Multipack selling' option

(1.1) Click 'Enable' in Advanced Settings tab of the offer details

(1.2) A guide table will be displayed after option is enabled, and the system will check the conditions. If Condition 1 is met, click "Create Campaign".
