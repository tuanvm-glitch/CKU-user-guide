# How to set up

AOV Bundle's Mix-match bundle feature offers an innovative way to create customizable bundles, giving your customers the freedom to mix and  match products they love while saving money. In this guide, you'll learn what the Mix-match bundle is, why it's a powerful tool for your store, and how to set it up effortlessly.

### What is Mix match bundle?&#x20;

Mix match bundle feature allows you to create flexible bundles where customers can select from a variety of products to create their perfect combination. It is recommended for stores with over 100 variants and up to three options. For example, you can offer a bundle where shoppers choose one chair, one table, and one lamp from a range of options to save on their total purchase.&#x20;

### Why Use Mix match Bundle?

<details>

<summary>Increase sales and boost AOV </summary>

By allowing customers to buy multiple products in a single transaction, you are encouraging them to make larger purchase, even including additional items they might not have thought to buy. Thereby, it can significantly increase your store AOV.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>Enhance customer experience</summary>

Customers often perceive bundles as more advantageous than individual items, as they are allowed to choose the products they prefer. They also have a feeling of control and personalization, thereby their shopping experience can be improved.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>Optimize inventory and Remove excess stock</summary>

&#x20;Providing mix-match bundle helps to promote slow-moving items as they can easily go with combination. Therefore, you can clear out inventory and make room for new products.&#x20;

</details>

### How to set up&#x20;

**Step 1: Navigate to the Mix match Bundle Feature**

Navigate **Offers** in the menu > **Create new offer >** Choose **Mix match bundle** and click **create** to start set up your first offer.&#x20;

#### Step 2: Configure Offer information&#x20;

**Offer title**

Enter a compelling offer title in the field. This title will be displayed to your customers on the storefront as the name of the bundle.&#x20;

#### Step 3: Set up Bundle option

A **bundle option** represents a group of products from which customers can select item. For each option added, customers are allowed to choose a single value (or product) corresponding to the quantity you’ve set.

For each **Bundle option** card, you can set uup:&#x20;

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
Option name

* Enter a clear label in **“Option name”**.
* This label appears in the right-hand **Product page preview** (e.g., _Option 1_, _Option 2_).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Select eligible products

* Click **“Select product”**.
* Pick the products customers may choose for this option.
* After you click **Select product** and choose items, each product appears in the option card with controls:
  * **Quantity box (right side):**\
    Type a number directly in the field to set how many units of that product a shopper will add when they pick it for this option.
  * **Edit variants (link under the product name):**\
    Click **Edit variants** to choose which variants of that product are eligible for this option (e.g., size, color). The text under the name updates (for example, **“1 of 3 variants selected”**).
  * **Remove (✕ icon):**\
    Click the **✕** to remove a product from this option’s list.
  * **Product count chip (next to Select product):**\
    Updates automatically to show how many products are currently eligible (e.g., **“3 item(s) selected”**).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Duplicate or delete an option**

* Click **“Duplicate”** to clone the option (keeps the name and product set; you can edit both).
* Click **“Delete”** to remove that option.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Check the **Product page preview** on the right at any time. Use the **Desktop** toggle there to review layout.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Repeat for many options by clicking the button **Add button option.**&#x20;
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

{% step %}
**Translations**

* Click **“Translation”** → pick a language → enter the translated Title, Description, and labels → **Add**.
* Keep variables like `{{discount_value}}` intact so the numbers remain dynamic.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Style (optional)**

* Click **“Edit widget”** to open the visual editor and adjust theme colors and label styles (same pattern as other features).
* Preview changes on the right; then **Save** inside the editor.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### Step 5: Schedule (when this offer runs)

Set **Start date** (calendar picker) and **time**.

To end the promotion, tick **“Set end date”** and choose an end date/time.

Times use your store timezone (shown above the fields).

#### Step 6: Final review & save

Scan the **Summary** in the right panel (if visible) and the **Product page preview** to ensure option names and selections look correct.

Confirm each **Bundle option** shows **“X item(s) selected”** (no red warnings).

Click **Save** (top of the page).

### Example setup

**“Build Your 3-Pack”**

* _Option 1_: select product(s) from “Shampoo” collection
* _Option 2_: select product(s) from “Conditioner” collection
* _Option 3_: select product(s) from “Styling” collection
* Widget label: **Save \{{discount\_value\}}**
* Schedule: start today at 10:00 PM, no end date

Customers will choose one product for each of the three options and see the savings badge update automatically.
{% endhint %}
