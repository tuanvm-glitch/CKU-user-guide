# Welcome

We’re so excited to have you here!🎉 AOV.ai Bundle is designed to help you **unlock more revenue from every order** by turning simple purchases into bigger, smarter bundles. With just a few clicks, you can create promotions that encourage customers to add more to their carts. Ready to boost your Average Order Value (AOV) while giving shoppers irresistible deals?&#x20;

### Why to use AOV.ai Bundle Upsell?

In today's competitive eCommerce landscape, customers expect not only convenience but also value for their money. This is where product bundles come in:&#x20;

### What to prepare before you start

To get the most out of AOV.ai Bundle Upsell, make sure you have:

* [ ] **Products and collections ready** in your Shopify store. You’ll assign these to bundles.
* [ ] **Clear goals in mind** (e.g., clear old stock, upsell premium items, or encourage bulk buying).
* [ ] **Basic discounts strategy** (percentage off, fixed value, or free gift).
* [ ] (Optional) **Brand styling preferences** (colors, fonts, button text), so your widgets match your storefront.
