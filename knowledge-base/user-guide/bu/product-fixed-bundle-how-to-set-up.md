# How to set up

Bundles are the powerful tools to increase your AOV and enhance customer satisfaction by allowing them to buy multiple items together at a discount. **Product Fixed Bundle** feature in the AOV Bundle Upsell app enables you to create predefined bundles that your customers will love. In this guide, we’ll explain what Product Fixed Bundles are, why they’re valuable, and how to set them up step-by-step.

### What is Product Fixed Bundle?&#x20;

The **Product Fixed Bundle** feature allows you to create a bundle with a predefined selection of products. Customers can purchase these bundles as a single package at a discounted price, boosting your sales while offering customers excellent value.

### Why to use Product Fixed Bundle?&#x20;

<details>

<summary>Increase Average Order Value</summary>

Compared to selling individual products, selling a curated selection of products together can increase the order value. Moreover, bundles which are offered at discounted pricing will encourage more customers to buy them.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>Streamline Inventory Management</summary>

Fixed Product Bundle can help reduce stockout, overstocking and streamline inventory management. It also helps to clear out old inventory, reduce warehouse storage costs and avoid supply chain inefficiencies.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>Enhance Customer Satisfaction</summary>

Fixed Product Bundle ensure easy purchase decision making, which is a key factor in driving sales and customer satisfaction. &#x20;

</details>

### How to Set up Product Fixed Bundle&#x20;

{% embed url="https://app.canvid.com/share/fi_01K3Q9KVD2S7HGG5P7YS162M9W" %}

#### Step 1: Create a new bundle

From your AOV.ai Bundle dashboard, go to **Offers → Create Product Fixed Bundle**.

#### Step 2: Enter offer information

**Offer title**: This will appear as the product name on your storefront. Choose a clear, descriptive title (e.g., _“Workout Essentials Bundle”_).

#### Step 3: Choose applicable products

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
Under **Applicable products**, click **Browse**.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Select up to **30 products** to include in your bundle.

Customers must purchase the entire selection—this is what makes it a “fixed” bundle.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Check the components info (on the right side panel):

* Max of 30 bundled products.
* Up to 3 product options.
* Up to 100 variants total.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### **Step 4: Configure offer details**&#x20;

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
**Discount method**: Choose how the discount is applied:

* **Percentage** (e.g., 15% off).
* **Fixed amount** (e.g., $20 off).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Value**: Enter the discount value.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Click **Apply** to set the discount.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Manage variants

Each Product Fixed Bundle is treated as a **single product variant** in your store. You can control how the bundle price is displayed here.

* **Original price**: This is automatically calculated by adding up the total price of all products in the bundle.
* **Displayed price**: This is the actual price customers will see on your storefront. You can adjust this to reflect your discount.

This makes it clear how much value they’re getting by purchasing the bundle instead of buying each product individually.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### **Step 5:** Show included products (optional)

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
#### Toggle **Show products included in bundle**.

This will display all child products (items inside the bundle) in a short list on the product page.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Content: Add the content of the product listed in bundle.&#x20;
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
Add translations (optional)

* Click the **Translation** button.
* Select a language from the dropdown.
* Enter translated content for the included products list.
  * Example: English → “All products included in bundle”
  * German → “Alle Produkte im Bundle enthalten”
* Click **Add** to save.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### Step 6: Save your product fixed bundle

Review your settings.

Check the **preview panel** on the right to confirm the bundle details.

Click **Save** to publish the Product Fixed Bundle to your store.

That’s it! You’ve created a **Product Fixed Bundle** 🎉. Now, customers can purchase your curated set of products at a discounted price, increasing your sales and delivering excellent value.
