## Step 1: Enable App Embed

Here are the ways you can embed the app into your theme:

*   **Method 1:** From the dashboard, click the **'Activate' button**.&#x20;

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F7YqVYj6fx1Eqe28bwM2w%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=9c632a57-090d-4658-a70c-d2eb20555b57" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    You will be redirected to the **Theme settings** where the app is automatically enabled. Then, simply click **'Save' button**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FindYdWZHg45Zs97ojVhD%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ef4f0ec3-65bd-426b-aee8-0f0d92cecb48" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Method 2:** From the onboarding, click the **'Enable' button**. Then, you will be also redirected to the **Theme settings** where the app is automatically enabled, just click **'Save' button**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F9x08zGVbeL1pjvVci8hc%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=4edd237e-9e97-4886-a3e8-5b340f0672c1" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Method 3:** From the **Checking Requirements** modal after successfully creating an offer, click the **'Enable app' button**. Then, follow the same steps as in **Method 2**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FEFtZujhmwrFymhGhnqrk%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=8e651da0-32ae-4109-b0e0-bd7ebffb35be" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Method 4:** From Shopify Admin, click **Online Store**. The Themes page will appear, then click **'Customize' button**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FtvCx34MZVqK0qnwjCuq1%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=33f5f29b-9cd3-4508-b191-8419ab8a150f" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    Then, 'Theme settings' page will display. On the left side of the screen, select '**App embeds' icon** or press **Window + Ctrl + 3**.&#x20;

    Next, find the **AOV Bundle** app, switch on the toggle, and finally click the '**Save' button**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F5QcDniqfqPqlfVWdhXIv%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=8b2440f5-ca3f-4feb-9549-69afbb83ea3e" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step 2: Add App Block

Here are the ways you can add the app block to  your theme:

*   **Method 1:** From the **Offer widget** section when creating an offer, click **'Add app block' button**.&#x20;

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F1j8h6FT6blsVPHy9QL3b%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a9d6a35f-2c94-4c8f-99c4-825c2fc35078" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    You’ll be redirected to **Theme settings** where the app block is automatically added as a placeholder. However, it will only appear if [the **AOV Bundle** app embed is enabled](#step-1-enable-app-embed).\
    You can drag the app block to your desired position, then click **'Save'** to apply your changes.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FBbj0Z8GllYEri4KogATH%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=33f3cd21-7807-4a65-bc56-3e30467d3082" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Method 2:** From the **Checking Requirements** modal after successfully creating an offer and enabling the app embed, click the **'Check app block' button**. \
    Then, follow the same steps as in **Method 1**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FPTzPBrmJHJSE9aBDtOOI%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=b81693de-e637-4b2f-93f9-65e573665e4a" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
*   **Method 3:** From Shopify Admin, click **Online Store**. The Themes page will appear, then click **'Customize' button**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FtvCx34MZVqK0qnwjCuq1%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=33f5f29b-9cd3-4508-b191-8419ab8a150f" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    Then, the **Theme settings** page will display. At the top of the screen, change **'Home page'** to **'Product page'.**

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F7BCdPODZ0rPpXWgiBZGA%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=0d7b4644-ecaa-4271-829b-03d56872f615" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    Once the **Product page** is displayed, on the left side of the screen, click **'Add block' button**, select the **App** tab, and choose the **'AOV Bundle' block**.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2F5PNP6dLn2QyF20UdZwZz%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=1949b7fa-901b-428e-a0c1-df9e75c2bd73" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    After adding the app block, you can drag it to your desired position. You’ll see the widget preview displayed as **a placeholder** in the bottom right corner. \
    Finally, click **'Save'** to apply your changes.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FuJeBbyX8aNgmRcrKi2tn%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=58fe4be7-c4b3-4a36-9a9c-afcea571bf42" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

    **Note:** To preview a specific campaign like the image above, change 'Default product' to the 'Trigger product' of an active offer.

    <figure><img src="https://645635813-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FUN6SVTvCdvGxor0uCeCx%2Fuploads%2FTYyj5qAuhcphppNMjDVg%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=bc18afab-6a5d-4cab-8322-e38f04cf0897" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



After successfully completing these two steps, try viewing it on your store. If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact support for free assistance.
