## Available integrations

### Judge.me

Display product reviews from [Judge.me](https://judge.me/) directly in your upsell offers using the **Testimonial widget**.

**Setup:**

1. Install Judge.me on your Shopify store
2. In the AOV Post Purchase Upsell app, go to **Integrations**
3. Connect your Judge.me account
4. In your offer's design editor, add the **Testimonial** widget

**Configuration options:**

| Setting       | Options                                   |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Review source | Judge.me                                  |
| Review type   | 5 stars only, 4 stars and up, All reviews |
| Sort by       | Recent, Highest rated                     |
| Layout        | Vertical, Horizontal                      |

### Air Reviews

Display product reviews from [Air Reviews](https://apps.shopify.com/air-reviews) in your upsell offers.

**Setup:**

1. Install Air Reviews on your Shopify store
2. In the AOV Post Purchase Upsell app, go to **Integrations**
3. Connect your Air Reviews account
4. Add the **Testimonial** widget in your offer's design editor

**Configuration options:** Same as Judge.me (review type, sorting, layout).

## Star Rating widget

In addition to full testimonials, you can show a **Star Rating** widget on your offers. This displays the product's average star rating.

**Customization:**

| Setting    | Options                   |
| ---------- | ------------------------- |
| Star color | Yellow, Blue, Pink, Black |
| Star size  | Small, Medium             |

The star rating appears near the product information and helps build trust at a glance.

## Tips

* **Social proof increases conversions** — offers with reviews and ratings typically perform 15-25% better
* Use the **5 stars only** filter for the strongest social proof
* The **Horizontal** layout works well for offers with limited vertical space
* Combine star ratings with testimonials for maximum impact
