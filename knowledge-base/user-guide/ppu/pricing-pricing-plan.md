# Pricing Plan

## How Pricing Works?

{% hint style="info" %}
**14-Day Free Trial**\
When you install the app, you’ll first receive a 14-day free trial with **all features fully unlocked**.

* During this period, no orders are counted toward billing.
* You won’t be charged while exploring all features.
* All paid features — including A/B testing, multi-language, and advanced targeting — are available from day one.
{% endhint %}

After the trial ends, billing works as follows:

* You’ll be billed based on the total number of orders your Shopify store processes in each 30-day cycle — even those unrelated to our app.
* Shopify automatically calculates and charges usage fees in your next billing invoice.
* Orders are tracked on a rolling 30-day window starting from the day your subscription begins (after the trial).
* The cap you agree to covers the highest possible pricing tier, but you’ll only pay for your actual usage.

***

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier          | Orders per 30 Days | Price     |
| ------------- | ------------------ | --------- |
| **Free**      | 0 – 100            | Free      |
| **Starter**   | 101 – 200          | $9.99/mo  |
| **Growth**    | 201 – 500          | $29.99/mo |
| **Scale**     | 501 – 1,000        | $49.99/mo |
| **Unlimited** | 1,001+             | $99.99/mo |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Maximum price cap: $99.99/mo**\
No matter how many orders your store processes, you will never be charged more than $99.99 per month.
{% endhint %}

### What Happens If I Unsubscribe?

If you cancel your subscription during a billing cycle:

* Your in-app upsell offers will no longer be shown to your storefront
* You will still be charged for the usage during your active days (after the trial)
* This charge will appear in your next Shopify invoice

**Example:**

* You subscribe on August 6 (after your trial ends)
* You uninstall the app on August 20
* From August 6 to August 20, your store received 310 orders
* On your next Shopify invoice (e.g., billed on September 5), you’ll be charged $29.99

{% hint style="warning" %}
If you uninstall the app, usage charges during your active subscription days will still apply.
{% endhint %}
