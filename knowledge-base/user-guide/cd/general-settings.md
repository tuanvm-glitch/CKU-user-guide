# General Settings

🛠️ General Settings

This section controls how and when the drawer opens, its width, and what happens when the cart is empty—crucial for UX.

Choose the setup options below by ticking the box:

* **Open on add to cart**: Automatically open drawer when an item is added.
* **Open on cart icon click**: Trigger drawer when the cart icon is clicked.
* **Show Continue Shopping button**: Add a button for users to keep browsing.

Cart width

* Choose **Slim**, **Normal**, or **Thick** for desktop display.

Empty cart content

* Customize text for empty cart state and the “Continue shopping” label.
* Redirect after continue shopping: Choose whether customers are sent to the collections page or a specific URL.
