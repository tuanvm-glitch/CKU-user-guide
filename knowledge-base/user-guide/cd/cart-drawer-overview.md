# Cart Drawer Overview

### What This Feature Does

The **Cart Drawer** lets customers see and update their cart in a slide-out panel instead of going to a separate cart page. It keeps shoppers on the product or collection they’re browsing while still making checkout only a click away. You control how it looks and behaves from one simple settings area.

### Why You'll Want This

* **Increase conversions** – Reduce friction by letting customers review their cart and head to checkout without losing their place.
* **Encourage larger orders** – A visible cart makes it easier for shoppers to keep adding products as they browse.
* **Create a smoother shopping experience** – Keep customers on the same page instead of bouncing them between product and cart pages.
* **Match your brand** – Customize the **Cart Title** and **Cart Width** so the cart drawer looks and feels like the rest of your store.

### Key Features

* **Cart Drawer Settings** – Manage all cart drawer options from one place, including layout, behavior, and key display choices.
* **Cart Title** – Set the heading shown at the top of the cart drawer (for example, “Your Cart” or “Shopping Bag”) to match your brand voice.
* **Cart Width** – Choose between **Slim**, **Normal**, or **Thick** to control how wide the cart drawer appears on your storefront.
* **Open on Add to Cart** – Automatically open the cart drawer as soon as a customer adds an item, so they instantly see what’s in their cart.
* **Show Continue Shopping Button** – Add a clear button that lets customers close the cart drawer and keep browsing your store.
* **Save Settings** – Update your cart drawer configuration with a single click, so changes go live on your storefront right away.

### How It Works (Customer Perspective)

When a shopper visits your store and adds a product to their cart, the **Cart Drawer** can slide in from the side of the screen. If you enable **Open on Add to Cart**, this happens automatically, so customers immediately see their selected items, order total, and a clear path to checkout.

Inside the cart drawer, customers can quickly review what they’ve added without leaving the page they were on. They can move on to checkout using your store’s checkout button, or, if you’ve enabled **Show Continue Shopping Button**, they can close the drawer and keep browsing products while their cart stays updated.

Because you control the **Cart Title** and **Cart Width**, the experience feels consistent with the rest of your branding. Whether you prefer a compact **Slim** layout or a bold **Thick** drawer, customers get a modern, focused view of their cart that keeps them engaged and moving toward purchase.

### When to Use This

* **You want a faster path to checkout** – Use the cart drawer when you want customers to move from product to checkout with fewer page loads and less friction.
* **You have browsing-heavy catalogs** – If shoppers often jump between collections and product pages, the cart drawer lets them keep track of their cart without losing their spot.
* **You’re optimizing for mobile** – A slide-out cart works especially well on phones and tablets, giving customers a clean, focused view of their order without a full-page cart.
