# Announcements

### What is the Announcements?&#x20;

The **Announcements** feature lets you show a time-sensitive message at the top of your cart drawer. With dynamic content and a countdown timer, it creates urgency, keeps users informed, and encourages faster checkout decisions. It is perfect for flash deals, limited-time offers, or stock countdowns.

### Why use the Announcements?&#x20;

<details>

<summary>Drive Urgency for Conversions</summary>

Add a countdown timer to communicate urgency and speed up decision-making at checkout.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Guide Buyer Attention</summary>

Place important messages right at the top of the cart drawer to ensure visibility.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Match Your Brand Aesthetic</summary>

Customize fonts, colors, and gradient backgrounds so announcements feel like part of your store design.

</details>

### How to set up the Announcements

You can activate and customize the feature directly from the **Body > Announcements** section in the Cart Drawer settings. Here, toggle the button at the top right of the settings to enable this feature.&#x20;

#### Step 1: Settings

**Announcement message**\
Write your message using plain text or formatting options:

* Use `**bold**` for bold text
* Use `*italic*` for italic text
* Use `{{timer}}` to automatically insert the countdown duration

**Timer settings**\
Set how many minutes the countdown lasts (e.g. `1` = one minute).

**Action when time’s up**\
Choose what happens when the countdown ends:

* _Hide the countdown_: Timer disappears after it hits zero
* _(Other actions may be supported in future updates)_

**Color**

**Gradient background**

Enable this option to apply a gradient effect across the announcement bar.

* Start color (e.g. `#E4FFC3`)
* End color (e.g. `#9BFBB3`)
