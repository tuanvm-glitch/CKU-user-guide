# Header

🧩**Header**

This is your opportunity to make the cart feel branded and dynamic.

* **Cart title**: Customize the title displayed at the top of the drawer. You can use variables like `{{cart_quantity}}` to show dynamic values (e.g., “Cart (2)”).
* **Alignment**: Choose **Left**, **Center**, or **Right** to position your title.
* **Height**: Choose between **Normal** or **Thick** padding for the header area.
