# Welcome

In today’s eCommerce landscape, every second counts. Shoppers expect seamless, fast, and modern experiences—and the traditional Shopify cart page just doesn’t meet those expectations. When customers are forced to leave their current page to view or edit their cart, it disrupts the buying journey and increases the chances of cart abandonment.

That’s where **AOV Cart Drawer** comes in.

### A Faster, Smarter Way to Checkout

AOV Cart Drawer replaces the default cart page with a sleek **slide-out cart drawer**. Instead of redirecting, your customers can instantly:

* Review items
* Update quantities
* Enter discount codes
* Leave order notes
* Head straight to checkout

All without leaving the product or collection page. The result? A smoother buying experience, **reduced cart abandonment**, and **higher conversion rates**.

### Discover Key Features

Here’s what you can explore right away:

🛒 **Slide-out cart drawer**\
Delivers a quick, modern checkout experience by keeping customers on the same page.

🔄 **Real-time cart updates**\
Instantly reflects changes to item quantities and pricing—no reloads, no confusion.

🏷️ **Discount code field (optional)**\
Let customers apply codes directly in the cart and see discounts reflected immediately.

📝 **Order note field (optional)**\
Give your customers space to leave specific instructions—great for gift messages, delivery preferences, or product customization.

🎨 **Customizable appearance**\
Change cart colors, fonts, and more to match your brand’s style—no coding required.

📱 **Fully responsive**\
Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile to provide a seamless experience across devices.
