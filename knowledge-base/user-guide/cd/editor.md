# Cart Editor

### What is Cart Editor?

**Cart Editor** is your control panel for customizing the look and behavior of your cart drawer. You can:

* Enable or disable the cart drawer
* Adjust cart appearance and layout
* Choose what info to show (discount code, order notes, etc.)

### How to set up&#x20;

#### Step 1: Navigate and activate Cart Editor&#x20;

From the AOV Cart Drawer app dashboard, select **Cart Editor** from the left-hand menu. Set the **Cart Status** to **Active** so it shows up on your storefront.\
\
Set up the cart information and appearance as desired. Then, click Save to confirm your changes.

#### Step 2: Customize your cart drawer&#x20;

The Cart Editor in AOV Cart Drawer gives you full control over what customers see and interact with in their cart. Let’s walk through each setting, section by section, so you can tailor your drawer exactly how you want.

🟢**Status**

* **Toggle drawer ON/OFF**: Control whether the cart drawer is active. This must be set to **Active** for the drawer to appear on your store.

#### Step 3: Start the setup

You’ve made it through enabling your cart drawer—nicely done! Now start to edit your cart, from styling your product cards to personalizing discount fields and order notes. Every little detail contributes to a smoother, more delightful shopping experience for your customers.

Happy optimizing, and cheers to higher conversions with AOV Cart Drawer Upsell!
